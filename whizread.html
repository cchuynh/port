WhizRead


Reimagination of a solution to the decline in reading rates with priorization of accessibility needs







ROLE

UX Researcher + Designer



COLLABORATORS

Other UX Researcher and Designer Teammates



TOOLS

Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Miro



DURATION

Sept 2023 - Dec 2023



OVERVIEW

College students need a fast and easy way to connect with a tutor when they are on a tight timeline.

If college students could be connected with a tutor within a short period of time, it would enhance studying experiences and be implemented throughout the higher education system.

Over the span of three weeks, I developed a deep passion for this case study which represents a revisionary approach to improve and gain my personal experience in UX Research and Design.
