get to know me!

i'm an INFJ, an pro at crocheting everything from clothes to plushies, and an addict to playing Word Hunt on iMessage.

To get quick intros out of the way: My name is Cecilia Huynh, and I'm currently a junior at the University of Michigan School of Information studying UX Design with a Ross Business Minor.


As a UX Designer, I offer a unique perspective on the application to my projects given my background in healthcare, both in research and application. In life and through my work, I strive to apply concepts of intentionality and empathy to fulfill my goals to improve accessibility through constant reliance on human-centered research and my prevailing desire to enhance digital and physical interactions in any and every industry.


Having a strong background in research, I always aim to first strengthen my interactions with others in order to perfect the designs I create to accommodate for their communicated needs and bridge the gap to make a difference in people's lives. Through my business endeavors, I have witness firsthand how the intersectionality between business and design relies on one another to help transform the world better than it was prior.
