To get introductions out of the way...

Hi there, I'm Cecilia! I'm a recent graduate from the University of Michigan School of Information and studied in UX Design and Business Administration.


My combined passions for art, storytelling, and psychology brought me here, in a position where I can help solve the everyday problems of users and empower them with delightful experiences and solutions.


As a UX Designer, I offer a unique perspective on the application to my projects given my background in healthcare, both in research and application. In life and through my work, I strive to apply concepts of intentionality and empathy to fulfill my goals to improve accessibility through constant reliance on human-centered research and my prevailing desire to enhance digital and physical interactions in any and every industry.


Having a strong background in research, I always aim to strengthen my interactions with others in order to perfect the designs I create to accommodate for their communicated needs and bridge the gap to make a difference in people's lives. Through my business endeavors, I have witness firsthand how the intersectionality between business and design relies on one another to help transform the world better than it was prior.
